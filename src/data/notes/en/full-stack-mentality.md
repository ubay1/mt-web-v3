# Full Stack Developer Mentality

Being full stack doesn't mean having to master everything instantly, but having the courage to **solve problems end-to-end**.

## Key Principles
- **User Empathy**: Don't just focus on backend performance, also consider the client side.
- **System Efficiency**: Minimize load on the client side with proper backend optimization.
- **Connectivity**: Understand the data flow from the database to the pixels on the user's screen.

### Learning Strategy
Focus on fundamental concepts first. Frameworks can change, but core principles like HTTP, Databases, and the DOM will always be relevant.
